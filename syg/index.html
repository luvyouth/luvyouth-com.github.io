    Luvyouth (Edge & Monty) Youth Group

    Luvyouth (Edge & Monty) Youth Group will be attending State Youth Games this year. 

    State Youth Games is held over the June Long Weekend (9th-12th June) at Lardner Park near Warragul.

    registration

    Registration involves 3 main steps.

    Registration needs to be filled our by parent/guardian unless you are over the age of 18+ (except for the sporting preferences)

    Please also read all information below the registration instructions as it informs you of costs, how to pay, what to bring etc.

     

     

     

     

    1) Complete SYG registration form

    Please fill in a registration form with SYG using the following link.

    Please note you only need to provide a WWCC Number if you are 18+.

    SYG Registration Form

    2) Check your inbox - choose sport preferences

    Soon after you have signed up with SYG you will receive a couple of emails that will allow you to log in to choose your sports preferences.









    3) Read the info below & stay tuned!

    Soon after you have signed up with SYG you will receive a couple of emails that will allow you to log in to choose your sports preferences.







    Camp info

    Cost

    Cost to attend SYG this year as part of Luvyouth is approximately as follows 

    Sports Participant    $100

    Team Helper             $60

    YG Leaders             $60

     

    This includes fees paid to SYG to run the event, camping site, transport and food. We understand this may be a large cost for some families. Please speak to youth pastor Tom if you need assistance with payment as we are seeking help from others in our church to help subsidise costs. There are also discounts available for multiple children in one family attending (see below).

     

    Payment

    A non-refundable deposit of $90 is due by the 15th May with the balance being due by the 26th May.

    Payments can be made via  Direct Deposit. BSB: 193-879 A/C: 441659222 - Youth Holding Account.

     

    Team Clothing

    We are required to wear a uniform. We are organising a hoodie and t-shirt package for approximately $50. - Check back soon for order form details.

     

    Sports 

     

    As mentioned above, your sports preferences are registered online. Once your preferences are loaded the leaders may need to make some alterations to complete teams for team events or due to logistical reasons. Where possible we will try to give you your first preference as well as give you an opportunity at both team and individual sports.

     

    Given the size of the event the best advice here is to make your sports selections as quickly as possible. Most of the sports are done on a first in basis.

    Camping Requirements

    Site will be organised by our wonderful helpers prior to arriving at SYG. Tents are provided! Refer to Packing guide for more info.

    Transport

    Meet at the church before 4pm on Friday 9th June 2023. 

     

    Important dates to remember

    Registrations Open                                          NOW

    Clothing Orders                                              15th May

    Deposit due ($90)                                           15th May
     

    Final Payment (including clothing ordered)     26th May

    Ensure medical details are up to date            2nd June

     

    What to bring 

     

    • Clothes - One thing we can guarantee is that it will be cold. Make sure you pack warm clothes.

    • Track pants, Jeans

    • Shorts (for sports)  

    • Tshirts, jumpers 

    • Some clothes that can get dirty

    • Underwear

    • Thermal underwear, skivvies

    • Extra socks (in case they get wet)

    • 3 pairs shoes 

    • Gumboots 

    • Rain coat

    • Beanie, gloves, scarf

    • Torch

    • Pj’s

     

    Other

    • Toiletries & towel

    • Sleeping Bag & Inner sheet (extra blanket if req’d)

    • Mattress & Pillow

    • Camp chair

    • Drink bottle
